Campus Life at UMD

The University of Minnesota Duluth campus is uniquely situated on a hillside overlooking Lake Superior, offering breathtaking views and a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern facilities. The campus features:

Academic Excellence

  • ✓ State-of-the-art Labovitz School of Business
  • ✓ Modern science and research facilities
  • ✓ Extensive library resources
  • ✓ Collaborative study spaces

Student Life

  • ✓ AMSOIL Arena for Bulldog Hockey
  • ✓ Superior Hiking Trail access
  • ✓ Active student organizations
  • ✓ Vibrant campus community

What makes UMD special is its perfect balance of academic rigor and recreational opportunities. The campus seamlessly connects to Duluth's extensive trail system, offering year-round outdoor activities from hiking to skiing. Inside, you'll find a warm and welcoming community despite Duluth's cold winters, with plenty of indoor connections between buildings making winter navigation comfortable.

The main reasons I chose to come to UMD are the beautiful environment and the nice campus. The nature around here is beauitiful, and the campus has pretty much everything you need, or at worst its close by.
