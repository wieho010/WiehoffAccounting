My Hobbies & Interests

Here are some of my favorite activities and interests that keep me engaged outside of academics and work.

Minnesota Wild Hockey

As a passionate Minnesota Wild fan, hockey has always been a significant part of my life. Whether it's watching games at the Grand Casino Arena or following our prospects, I'm always engaged with the team. The energy of live hockey games and the community of Wild fans make every game an unforgettable experience, win or loss.

The Great Outdoors

Minnesota summers are best spent on the water. Whether it's enjoying a peaceful morning fishing session or spending time with family and friends on the boat, the lake life is an essential part of my life. The Fourth of July celebrations on the water are particularly special, with boat parades and fireworks displays.

Video Games

I have always been a huge gamer. Ever since I first played on the Xbox 360, I was hooked. My parents got me a 3ds and the game Pokemon Black for my 7th birthday. This only pushed me to love gaming further, and I still do it consistently to this day. Nowadays I play single player games and a few multiplayer shooters, with my favorite being CounterStrike:Global Offensive
